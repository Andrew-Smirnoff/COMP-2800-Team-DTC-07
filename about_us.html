Hi, and welcome to Fiendship! Fiendship is an app designed to strengthen the bond between you and your group of friends. We are all sudents at BCIT and all made this project together. When asked to make an app about friendship, we almost immediately thought of this idea and got to work for the next five weeks designing, programming and testing it. After many days of hard work, it is finally complete. We hope you and your friends enjoy, but be warned, as some friends will never be the same!
